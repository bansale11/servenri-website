Property Management

Complete on-ground management for your India properties, tenant relations, rent collection, maintenance, compliance, and regular reporting. You stay informed while we handle everything.

Tenant Management & Rent Collection
Rigorous tenant screening, lease execution, and timely rent collection with monthly digital statements remitted to your overseas account.
Maintenance & Repairs
